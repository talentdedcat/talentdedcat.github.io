Electronic Design Automation / Efficient Hardware

Shizhang Wang 王诗章

Researcher in AI for EDA and low-power optimization

I am an early-career researcher working at the intersection of artificial intelligence and electronic design automation. My work focuses on AI for EDA, efficient hardware design, and low-power optimization, with particular interest in making chip design and accelerator development faster, more reliable, and more energy-efficient.

I have contributed to peer-reviewed work published at ICCAD, ASP-DAC, MLCAD, ACM TODAES, and IEICE Electronics Express. Our work on agile LLM accelerator development received the ACM/IEEE William J. McCalla ICCAD Best Paper Award in the Front-End category.
