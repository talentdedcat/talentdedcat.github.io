# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

The primary audience is faculty, researchers, graduate students, and potential collaborators in electronic design automation and low-power integrated-circuit design. They typically arrive from a paper, conference program, search result, or GitHub profile and need to establish identity, affiliation, research focus, and publication credibility quickly.

## Product Purpose

Present Shizhang Wang (王诗章) as an early-career researcher working at the intersection of AI for EDA and low-power optimization. The site should make verified publications, academic affiliations, awards, and scholarly identifiers easy to scan and follow while remaining simple to maintain and deploy through GitHub Pages.

## Brand Personality

Precise, restrained, research-driven. The voice is confident but factual, using concrete venues, links, and contribution areas instead of promotional superlatives.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic academic templates, card-heavy SaaS layouts, neon cyberpunk styling, decorative glass effects, fake charts, unverified citation metrics, oversized marketing copy, and claims that cannot be traced to a public source or directly confirmed by the author.

## Design Principles

1. Evidence before decoration: publications, DOI links, affiliations, and awards carry the page.
2. Make research legible: explain the through-line between AI for EDA, efficient hardware, and low-power optimization.
3. Reward scanning: visitors should understand identity, research focus, and recent work within the first viewport.
4. Maintain authorship integrity: distinguish verified records from uncertain same-name matches.
5. Stay easy to update: keep content in structured data with no build step.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA contrast and keyboard access. Respect reduced-motion preferences, avoid color-only meaning, provide descriptive labels and alternative text, and support mobile widths down to 320 pixels without horizontal scrolling.
